How it works

Subscribe once, get entered every week, and support UK charities along the way.

Steps

1. Subscribe for £10/month via Stripe (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, Mastercard).
2. Your active subscription = one entry in each weekly draw during your paid month.
3. Draw every Friday at 8pm (UK). We publish an anonymised winner ID after verification.
4. We verify winners (18+ and UK residency) and pay the prize by bank transfer.
5. Each month we publish donation totals for our 10 supported UK charities.

FAQ

Who can play?

UK residents only, aged 18+.

How many entries do I get?

One active subscription = one entry in every weekly draw during your paid month.

How do I cancel?

Use the link in your Stripe receipt to manage or cancel anytime.

How are winners selected?

Randomised selection using an auditable method. If a technical error occurs, we re-run the draw.

When are donations made?

We batch donations and publish monthly totals on the Winners & donations page.

Eligibility & responsible play
