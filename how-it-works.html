How it works

Subscribe, choose your charity, and you’re in the weekly draw. Results and donations are published for full transparency.

1. Subscribe

£10/month via secure Stripe checkout. You’ll receive a receipt with a manage/cancel link.

2. Choose your charity

Pick from our supported UK charities during checkout. We save your selection.

3. Weekly draw

Each Friday 8pm, one winner is chosen from active, paid subscribers.

4. Publish results

Winner ID is posted on the Winners page. We verify eligibility before paying out.

5. Donate monthly

We aggregate donations monthly and publish receipts on the Transparency page.

6. Responsible play

UK residents only, 18+. Please play responsibly. Support links on the Legal page.
