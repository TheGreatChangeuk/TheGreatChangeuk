Exactly How It Works

Subscribe for £10/month. 60% fuels the weekly prize pot, 30% goes to the charity you choose, and 10% covers operations. Here’s the full process, end‑to‑end.

Where Your £10 Goes

£6 → Prize Pot (60%)

Directly funds the weekly prize. Pot size varies with active subscribers.

£3 → Charity (30%)

Donated monthly to your chosen charity. Tracked on our Transparency page.

£1 → Operations (10%)

Keeps things running (payments, hosting, fees) so the model is sustainable.

Note: Prize values vary with the size of the pot and are not guaranteed.

Weekly Draw Process

  1. Eligibility snapshot: we take all active, paid subscribers at the weekly cut‑off ([Day] [Time]).
  2. Random selection: we run a fair random draw from that list and keep an audit log.
  3. Notify winner: we contact the winner by email.
  4. Verification: winner may be asked to verify age/ID before payout.
  5. Payout: prize is paid via UK bank transfer.

If we’re unable to verify or reach the winner within 30 days, the prize may be forfeited as set out in our Terms.

Eligibility

  • 18+ and UK resident.
  • One account per person; duplicate/fraudulent accounts may be closed.
  • Employees involved in administering the draw are not eligible.

Full details in our Terms & Conditions.
