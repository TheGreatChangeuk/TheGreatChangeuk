How it works

Subscribe, choose your charity, and you’re in the weekly draw. We publish winners and donation reports regularly.

1. Subscribe

£10/month via secure Stripe checkout. Your receipt includes a manage/cancel link.

2. Choose your charity

Pick from our 10 supported UK charities during checkout.

3. Weekly draw

Every Friday 8pm we select one winner from active, paid subscribers.

4. Publish results

Winner ID is posted on the Winners section after verification.

5. Donate monthly

Monthly totals and proofs are posted on the Transparency section.

6. Responsible play

UK residents only, 18+. Please play responsibly.

FAQ

Who can subscribe?

UK residents aged 18+.

How do I manage or cancel?

Use the link in your Stripe receipt, or contact us at support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

When is the draw?

Every Friday at 8pm. Winner ID is posted on the Winners section after verification.

How are donations handled?

We aggregate monthly and publish totals and proof references in Transparency.

Which charities can I pick?

We currently support 10 trusted UK charities. You choose during checkout.
