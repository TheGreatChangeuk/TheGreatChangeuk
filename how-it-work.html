How it works

Subscribe, choose your charity, and you’re in the weekly draw. We publish winners and donation reports regularly.

1. Subscribe

£10/month via secure Stripe checkout. Your receipt includes a manage/cancel link.

2. Choose your charity

Pick from our 10 supported UK charities during checkout.

3. Weekly draw

Every Friday 8pm we select one winner from active, paid subscribers.

4. Publish results

Winner ID is posted on Winners after verification.

5. Donate monthly

We publish monthly totals and proof references in Transparency.

6. Responsible play

UK residents only, 18+. Please play responsibly.

FAQ

Who can subscribe?

UK residents aged 18+.

How do I manage or cancel?

Use the link in your Stripe receipt, or email support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

When is the draw?

Every Friday at 8pm (UK time).

How are donations handled?

We allocate funds monthly and publish totals and proof references on our site.

Which charities can I pick?

10 trusted UK charities. You choose during checkout.
