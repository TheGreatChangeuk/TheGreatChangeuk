How it works

Subscribe, choose your charity, and you’re in the weekly draw. We publish winners and donation reports regularly.

£10/month UK residents only 18+

1) Subscribe

Secure Stripe checkout (Apple Pay / Google Pay). Your receipt includes a manage/cancel link.

2) Choose charity

Pick from our 10 supported UK charities during checkout.

3) Weekly draw

Every Friday 8pm we randomly select a winner from active, paid subscribers.

Billing & management

£10 billed monthly. Update payment method or cancel anytime via the self-service link in your Stripe receipt.

Eligibility

UK residents only, 18+. One active subscription = one entry in each weekly draw.

Winner verification

We verify age and UK residency before paying prizes. Winner IDs are posted publicly after verification.

Donations & transparency

We publish monthly donation totals (by charity) on the Winners & donations page.

FAQ

Who can subscribe?

UK residents aged 18+.

How do I cancel or change my details?

Use the self-service link in your Stripe receipt, or email support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

When is the draw?

Every Friday at 8pm (UK time). Winner ID is posted after verification.

Which charities can I pick?

We currently support 10 trusted UK charities. You choose during checkout.

How are donations allocated?

We aggregate monthly and publish totals and proof references on the Winners & donations page.
