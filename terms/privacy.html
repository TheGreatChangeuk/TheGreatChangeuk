Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 September 2025

1. Who we are

The Great Change UK Ltd is the controller for the personal data we process. Contact: support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

2. What data we collect

CategoryDetails
Account & billingName, email, billing address (if provided), transaction history, and payment tokens handled by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers.
Winner verificationAge and UK residency evidence (e.g., ID check outcome). We record that verification occurred (and the result), not the full document image unless necessary.
CommunicationsMessages you send to support and our replies.
TechnicalEssential cookies (consent choice), basic logs for security and availability.

3. Why we use your data (legal bases)

  • Contract: to run subscriptions, weekly draws, and pay prizes.
  • Legal obligations: age/residency checks for winners, anti-fraud and record-keeping.
  • Legitimate interests: site security, fraud prevention, reporting, supporting customers.
  • Consent: only for non-essential cookies/marketing (not used by default).

4. Sharing your data

We share data with trusted processors only as needed:

  • Stripe (payments, fraud prevention)
  • Hosting and email providers
  • Verification partners for age/residency if required

Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses/UK IDTA).

5. Retention

  • Transactions and finance records: retained per statutory requirements.
  • Verification logs: retained for compliance and audit, then minimised or deleted.
  • Support emails: retained as needed to manage the service.
  • Cookie consent choice: up to 12 months.

6. Your rights

You can request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing where applicable. To exercise your rights, contact support@thegreatchangeuk.com. You can also complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies to make the site work and remember your consent. See our Cookie Notice for details. If we introduce analytics/marketing cookies later, we’ll ask for consent first.

8. Security

We use technical and organisational measures appropriate to the risk (TLS encryption, access controls, logging, and data minimisation). Payment details are handled by Stripe.

9. Children

Our service is for UK residents aged 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted here with a new date. Significant changes may also be notified by email where appropriate.

11. Contact

Email: support@thegreatchangeuk.com
