Who we are

The Great Change UK is the controller for your personal data. Contact: support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

Data we collect

  • Account & billing (via Stripe): name, email, card token/last4, billing country, transaction history.
  • Verification: age and UK residency checks (documents or third-party checks) before paying prizes.
  • Communications: emails you send to us and our responses.
  • Site data: essential cookies/logs for security and consent. We do not currently use analytics or marketing cookies.

Why we use your data (lawful bases)

  • Contract: operate subscriptions, run weekly draws, contact winners, pay prizes.
  • Legal obligation: age/residency verification, financial record keeping, fraud prevention.
  • Legitimate interests: security, service improvement, reporting, responding to enquiries.
  • Consent: only if we introduce non-essential cookies or direct marketing (not currently used).

Sharing

We share data with processors including Stripe (payments), email and hosting providers, and verification vendors (where required). We do not sell your personal data.

International transfers

If processors transfer data outside the UK/EEA, we rely on appropriate safeguards (such as UK IDTA/SCCs).

Retention

  • Billing and transaction records: as required by tax/financial laws.
  • Winner verification records: for required legal/accounting periods.
  • Support communications: as needed for operations and complaints handling.

Your rights

You can request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability where applicable. You also have the right to complain to the ICO: ico.org.uk.

Marketing

We do not currently send marketing emails. If this changes, we will use opt-in consent and provide easy unsubscribe.
