Cookie Notice

How we use cookies and similar technologies — and how you can control them.

Last updated: . You can change your choice at any time using the button below.

1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. We also use similar storage (like localStorage) for the same purposes.

2) How we use cookies

Right now, we only use essential cookies/storage to make the site work (for example, remembering whether you accepted or declined cookies). If we add analytics in the future (e.g., Google Analytics 4), we will only use them with your consent.

3) Categories

  • Essential — required for the site to function (e.g., your cookie preference). These do not require consent.
  • Analytics (optional) — help us understand usage to improve the site. We will not load these unless you choose “Accept”.
Current status: No analytics cookies are set by default on this site. Only essential storage is used.

4) What we set

Name / KeyTypePurposeExpiry
cookieConsent (localStorage) Essential Stores your cookie banner choice: accepted or declined, so we don’t show the banner again. Until you clear it / “Reset my choice”
Session storage keys Essential May be used by the site to keep navigation state while you browse pages. End of session

5) Third-party cookies

If you proceed to checkout, Stripe may set its own cookies on Stripe-hosted pages to enable payments and prevent fraud. Those are controlled by Stripe’s policies on their domain.

You can change your cookie choice at any time:

You can also control cookies via your browser settings (blocking, clearing). If you block essential storage, parts of the site may not work.

7) Changes to this notice

We may update this notice to reflect changes in law or our use of cookies. We’ll update the date above and describe material changes where appropriate.
