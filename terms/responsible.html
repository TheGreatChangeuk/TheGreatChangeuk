Responsible Gambling

18+ · UK residents only. Please play responsibly.

Stay in control

  • Set a monthly budget you can afford.
  • Keep play fun — not a way to make money.
  • Don’t chase losses; take regular breaks.
  • Track your spend; consider limiting play time.

Self-exclusion & account closure

We can help you pause or stop play.

  • Self-exclusion: Email support@thegreatchangeuk.com with subject “Self-Exclusion”. We’ll confirm and disable entries for your chosen period (min. 6 months if preferred).
  • Account closure: Ask us to close your account at any time.

During self-exclusion, we take reasonable steps to prevent re-subscription.

Support & advice

  • BeGambleAware — 0808 8020 133 — begambleaware.org
  • GamCare — 0808 8020 133 — gamcare.org.uk
  • National Gambling Helpline — 24/7 freephone 0808 8020 133
  • Consider device-level blocking tools and bank gambling blocks, where available.

Age verification

We verify prize winners (18+ and UK residency) before paying prizes. If verification fails, entries and prizes may be void.

Risk information

Weekly draws are games of chance — outcomes are random and cannot be predicted. Only play if you can afford to lose.

Contact us

If you’re concerned about your play or someone else’s, email support@thegreatchangeuk.com — we’ll respond with options and support resources.
