Responsible Gambling

18+ · UK residents only. Please play responsibly.

Play safely

  • Set a monthly budget you can afford; never chase losses.
  • Keep play fun — not an income source.
  • Take breaks and review spending regularly.
  • Contact us to close or self-exclude your account at any time.

Self-exclusion & account closure

Email support@thegreatchangeuk.com with the subject “Self-Exclusion”. We will confirm by email and disable entries for the exclusion period you request (minimum 6 months if you prefer a formal exclusion; immediate closure available on request).

Age & residency checks

We verify winners (18+ and UK residency) before paying prizes. If verification fails, entries and prizes may be void.
