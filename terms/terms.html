1. Who we are

“The Great Change UK” (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a company registered in England & Wales (company number [Company number]; registered office [Registered office]). Contact: support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

Regulatory status: We operate a subscription-based weekly prize draw model. If licensing or regulatory registration becomes applicable to our exact product structure, we will update this page and any disclosures accordingly.

2. Definitions

  • Subscription: a monthly recurring payment that confers one entry to each weekly draw within the paid period.
  • Draw: a random selection conducted on a scheduled date to determine a prize winner.
  • Winner: the person whose entry is selected in a draw, subject to verification.
  • Active: subscription payment successfully collected and not cancelled/suspended at draw time.

3. Eligibility & verification

  • You must be a UK resident aged 18+.
  • We may require proof of age and UK residency at any time. Failure to pass verification voids entries and prizes.
  • You agree not to allow others (including anyone under 18) to use your account or enter on your behalf.

4. Subscriptions, pricing & payment

  • Price: £10 per month, processed by Stripe (Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, Mastercard).
  • One active subscription equals one entry to each weekly draw in the applicable billing month.
  • Manage/cancel via the link in your Stripe receipt. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
  • If a payment fails and is not resolved by the draw cutoff, that week’s entry is not eligible.

5. Draw schedule & selection

  • Draws occur weekly on Fridays at 20:00 (UK time). We may adjust the schedule with notice.
  • Eligible pool: all active, paid subscriptions as of the draw time.
  • Selection method: randomised process using an auditable method. If a technical fault occurs, we may rerun the draw.
  • We publish an anonymised winner ID after verification.

6. Winners, verification & prize fulfilment

  • We contact the winner using the email associated with the subscription checkout.
  • Verification: age (18+) and UK residency. We may request additional documents where legally required (e.g., AML checks).
  • Upon successful verification, prizes are paid by bank transfer (or a mutually agreed method) within 10 working days.
  • If we cannot contact or verify the winner within 28 days, we may select a reserve winner or carry the prize forward.
  • Publicity: we may publish an anonymised winner ID. Names/images are used only with consent.

7. Donations & charities

Subscribers can select one of our supported UK charities during checkout. We publish monthly donation totals on our Winners & donations page. Charity partners may change over time.

8. Prohibited conduct & misuse

We may void entries, withhold or recover prizes, suspend or close accounts if we reasonably suspect: fraud, abuse, multiple accounts to bypass limits, automated/robotic entries, or breaches of these terms.

9. Cancellations & refunds

Subscriptions are billed monthly in advance. Refunds are not typical unless required by law or due to our error. Cancellation takes effect at the next billing cycle; any remaining draws in the paid period remain eligible.
