Allocation of proceeds

We allocate net proceeds from subscriptions as follows:
50% to prize funds  •  30% to charitable donations  •  20% to operating costs (including payment processing, compliance, insurance, hosting and administration).

Our public pages state “at least 20% to charity.” This section provides the full allocation figure for transparency.
