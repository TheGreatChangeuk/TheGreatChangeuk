Responsible Gambling

We want participation to be fun and responsible.

Play responsibly

  • Only spend what you can afford. Set a monthly limit and stick to it.
  • Keep it recreational — not a way to make money.
  • Don’t chase losses. Take breaks if needed.
  • Keep your details secure and don’t let others use your subscription.

Get support

  • BeGambleAware.org — advice and tools
  • GamCare — free support (0808 8020 133)
  • GAMSTOP — free self-exclusion service

Self-exclusion / pauses

If you wish to self-exclude or pause your participation, contact support@thegreatchangeuk.com. We will action requests as quickly as possible and confirm by email.

Cookie Notice

We use cookies and similar technologies to help our site work and to understand how it’s used. You can manage your preferences via the cookie banner or your browser.

Types of cookies

  • Strictly necessary — required for core functionality (e.g. security, navigation, forms).
  • Analytics (optional) — help us improve the service (e.g. aggregated usage stats). Used with your consent.
  • Payment — Stripe may set cookies at checkout for fraud prevention and session continuity.

Managing cookies

  • Use our cookie banner to accept/reject non-essential cookies.
  • You can also control cookies in your browser settings.

Example cookies (indicative)

  • tgcu_session — strictly necessary, remembers your navigation during a visit, expires when you close the browser.
  • _ga (if enabled) — analytics, helps count visits and traffic sources, retention per provider policy.
  • Stripe checkout cookies — fraud prevention and payment session (see Stripe’s policy).

Contact us at support@thegreatchangeuk.com for any cookie questions.
