Frequently asked questions

Short, clear answers to the most common questions.

Who can subscribe?

UK residents aged 18+.

How do I manage or cancel?

Use the “Manage subscription” link in your Stripe receipt, or contact us.

When is the draw?

Every Friday at 8pm. Winner ID is posted on the Winners page after verification.

How are donations handled?

We aggregate monthly and publish totals on the Transparency page, including proof of donation references.

Which charities can I pick?

We currently support 10 trusted UK charities. You choose during checkout.
