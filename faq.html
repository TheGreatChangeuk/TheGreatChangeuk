Frequently asked questions

Quick answers about eligibility, subscriptions, charity donations and the weekly prize draw.

Who can subscribe?

18+ UK residents only. One subscription per person. You must be physically in the UK when purchasing.

How do I choose my charity?

You select your UK charity during Stripe checkout. Your choice is saved to your subscription and used for monthly donations.

How much goes to charity?

At least 20% of proceeds are donated to UK good causes chosen by subscribers. See monthly proof on our Transparency page.

When are prizes drawn?

We select one winner each week from active, paid subscribers at the weekly cutoff, then publish once verified.

What is the prize amount?

The weekly prize varies based on the number of active subscribers. See the current estimate on our home page.

How do I cancel?

Use the “Manage subscription” link on your Stripe receipt at any time, or email support@thegreatchangeuk.com.

Payment methods

Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa and Mastercard via Stripe.

What personal details are shown for winners?

With consent, we may publish first name + initial, town/county, and prize amount.

Responsible play

Please play responsibly. If you need support: BeGambleAware.org (0808 8020 133).

I need help or want to complain

Email support@thegreatchangeuk.com. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.
